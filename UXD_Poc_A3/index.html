Food Tracker

What did you eat today?

The food we consume contributes to climate change. The Food Tracker provides the opportunity to calculate the resultant carbon dioxide from the food you eat, called your “Food Carbon Footprint” and the amount of water consumed in your foods production called your “Food Water Footprint”.

About us

We use our technical expertise and practical understanding to protect nature and defend the rights of communities to a healthy environment. We act as advisers to the environment movement and we work with community based environment groups, regional and state environmental organisations. We seek out new and innovative ways to tackle environmental problems.

Our mission

Expose global environmental problems and to promote solutions that are essential to a green and peaceful future. Everything on this planet is symbiotically connected-plants, animals, humans and the Earth itself. As individuals we care about many different causes, but in our concern for the fate of our children and our Earth, we are united. We share one home-Earth. It’s our mission to save it.
